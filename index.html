About SEIA & SROI

This tool helps you calculate Social and Environmental Impact Assessment (SEIA) and Social Return on Investment (SROI). Add indicators relevant to the project, define proxies with monetary values, and assign stakeholder weights (1-10) to prioritize different impacts. The multiplier system ensures that higher-priority stakeholder concerns receive appropriate weighting in the final impact calculation.

Multipliers Logic

  • Weight 1-3: Multiplier = 1.0 (Low priority)
  • Weight 4-7: Multiplier = 1.25 (Medium priority)
  • Weight 8-10: Multiplier = 1.5 (High priority)
